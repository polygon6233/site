See how your data is managed

At Polygon Creations, we take your data privacy and security seriously. We believe in transparency and want to ensure you have a clear understanding of how we manage your data. This document outlines our data management practices to help you make informed decisions about your personal information.

Data Collection

We only collect the necessary information required to provide you with our services and improve your overall experience. This includes basic personal information such as name, email address, and contact details. We may also collect non-personal information, such as website usage data, to better understand how our platform is being utilized.


Purpose of Data Collection

The data we collect serves specific purposes, including but not limited to:

  1. Facilitating smooth account registration and login processes.
  2. Providing personalized services and content based on your preferences.
  3. Sending important updates and notifications related to our services.
  4. Conducting internal research and analysis to enhance our offerings.
  5. Complying with legal requirements and obligations.

Data Storage and Security

Rest assured, we maintain strict security measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your information is stored on secure servers and accessible only to authorized personnel who have a legitimate need to access it.


Data Sharing

We will never sell any of your data to third-party services. However, in some cases, we may share certain information with trusted service providers and partners who assist us in delivering our services. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are only granted access to the data necessary to perform their designated tasks.


Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this document or as required by applicable laws. Once your data is no longer needed, we will securely dispose of it.


Your rights

As a user, you have the right to access, update, correct, or delete your personal information stored in our systems. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this document.


Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand how you interact with our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.


Updates to this Policy

Our data management practices may evolve over time. We will notify you of any material changes to this policy, and you will have the opportunity to review and consent to these updates if required by law.


By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood our data management practices outlined in this document. We appreciate your trust in Polygon Creations and remain committed to protecting your privacy.
